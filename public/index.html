Voice Chunker

Tag dialogue by speaker, run find/replace, and split it into size-capped chunks.

▸ Splitting & voice options
A single space by default.
Leave blank for no limit.
{name} is replaced with the speaker. Dialogue follows after a space.

Voices & switches: each NAME: tag is a voice; a switch is when the speaker changes between two lines inside one chunk. Set a cap in the options, or leave it blank for no limit. Untagged lines are a narrator voice and don't count toward the limit unless you enable it.

Continued lines: when one line is too long for a single chunk, it's cut on word boundaries and every continuation re-opens with the speaker name so the right voice carries over.

Privacy: everything runs in this page; nothing you paste leaves your device. (Self-hosting? Add localStorage if you want it to remember your rule presets.)
